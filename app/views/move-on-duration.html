Placement duration and move on

What is the duration of placement required?

We'll use the number of weeks to calculate the date the person will be expected to leave the Approved Premises (AP), if they're accepted.

How to decide the duration of an AP placement

Standard AP placements typically last a maximum of 12 weeks but can be longer in exceptional circumstances where required for risk management purposes.



ESAP placements can last up to 52 weeks. PIPE placements usually last up to 26 weeks.
