Use this service to apply for a placement in an AP on behalf of an individual who'll be on probation.

If an individual is leaving prison, you should aim to complete your application 6 months before they are released.

You can leave and return to your application at any time. Your progress will be saved.

Before you start

You need

  • the individual’s CRN
  • an up to date OASys. We'll use this to pre-fill some of your application
  • the oral hearing date, if applicable and if known

You need to know:

  • eligibility requirements and why an AP is necessary over other interventions and accommodation options
  • factors that will have an impact on the location and which type of AP and room will be suitable
  • how you'll prepare the person for move on after the AP placement
