Use this service to apply for a placement in an approved premises (AP) on behalf of an individual who'll be on probation.

If an individual is leaving prison, you should aim to complete your application 6 months before they are released.

You can leave and return to your application at any time. Your progress will be saved.

Before you start

You need

  • an up to date OASys – we'll use this to pre-fill some of your application
  • the individual’s CRN
  • the oral hearing date, if applicable and if known

You need to understand:

  • eligibility requirements - you'll need to demonstrate why an AP is necessary over other interventions and accommodation options
  • factors impacting which location, type of AP, and type of room will be suitable
  • how you'll prepare for move on after the AP
