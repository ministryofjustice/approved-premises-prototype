Apply for an Approved Premises (AP) placement

Use this service to apply for a placement in a male or female Approved Premises (AP).

Approved Premises support the risk management of individuals who pose a very high / high risk of serious harm and have complex needs. When applying for an Approved Premises placement you must identify how the placement will contribute to risk management.

Applications should not be made solely for accommodation needs. Where appropriate, consider alternatives such as CAS2 or CAS3

Applications should be completed 6 months before a person leaves prison. There are some exceptions, for example if the sentence is shorter than 6 months or there has been a change in risk or circumstances.

You can save your progress and return to your application at any time.

Before you start

You need:

  • the person's CRN
  • an up to date OASys assessment completed within the last 6 months - this is required in order for the service to import OASys information

You need to know:

  • eligibility requirements - you'll need to demonstrate why an AP is necessary over other interventions and accommodation options
  • factors impacting which location, type of AP, and type of room will be suitable
  • how you'll prepare for move on after the AP
! Warning

The person's OASys record must be updated before starting an application.

If you start an application and then update the OASys record, any changes will not be imported. You will need to restart the application from the beginning for the changes to be imported.
