How will an Approved Premises (AP) placement support the management of risk?

AP placements provide the following support, restrictions, and monitoring as standard:

  • 24 hour staff support and monitoring
  • Overnight curfew (from 11pm until 6am)
  • CCTV
  • Room searches
  • Rehabilitative interventions (formerly purposeful activities)
  • Medication monitoring
  • Drug and alcohol testing

AP placements can also provide additional measures if needed. For example:

  • Additional curfews or sign-ins at specific times or intervals
  • Trigger plans
  • Regular drug and alcohol testing
  • Monitoring the possession of electronic devices
