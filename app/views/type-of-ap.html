What are the different types of AP?

Standard APs provide 24 hour staff support and monitoring, an overnight curfew, CCTV, room searches, rehabilitative interventions, medication monitoring, and drug and alcohol testing.

Psychologically informed planned environment (PIPE) APs are for men who screen into the Offender Personality Disorder (OPD) pathway. PIPE staff have additional training to manage individuals and create an enhanced safe and supportive environment.

Enhanced Security AP (ESAP) offer additional security measures and are intended to individuals whose risk management plan and ability to manage in the community are reliant on those measures.
