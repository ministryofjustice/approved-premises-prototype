Which type of AP does Robert require?

What are the different types of AP?

PIPE (psychologically informed planned environment) APs are for men who screen into the OPD (offender personality disorder) pathway. PIPE staff have additional training to manage individuals and create an enhanced safe and supportive environment.

ESAPs (enhanced security APs) offer additional security measures and are intended for individuals whose risk management plan and ability to manage in the community are reliant on those measures.
